About Me

Hailing from northern Idaho and living in Portland, Oregon currently, I know a thing or two about staying warm during the winter months. Instead of wearing layered shirts or hoodies, I have always preferred to wear sweaters in the winter because they are super cozy, generally soft, and come in unique color patterns or knit sytles. My collection grows as I realize just how versatile sweaters are. I have chosen to highlight a few of my own sweaters, providing a few specs about each as well as a mini-interview that reflects their individual personality. It is my hope that fellow sweater enthusiasts and prospective wearers will be inspired to make some great memories with a sweater of their own.

- Harrison
